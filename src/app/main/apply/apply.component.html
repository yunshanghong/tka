How to Apply

Apply for a KINTO One vehicle in just 4 easy steps.

1

Choose the Car That’s Right for You

Start by choosing the car you like and that fits your needs—then decide how many years you want to drive it for.
2

Order online

Fill up your personal information and place your order.
3

Confirmation

After we confirm your order and your credit is approved, you’ll receive an email link to pay the security deposit and we’ll arrange a date to deliver the car to your doorstep.
4

Just Drive!

You’ve taken delivery of your new Lexus or Toyota. Just add petrol to start enjoying your dream car!
